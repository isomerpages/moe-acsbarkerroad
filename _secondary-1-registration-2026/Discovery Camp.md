#### **Secondary 1 Discovery Camp 2026 – “Beacon of Truth & Light”** ####
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


As part of your son/ward’s orientation and initiation into the traditions and culture of ACS (Barker Road), he is required to attend the Secondary 1 Discovery Camp. This will be an excellent time for your son to strengthen his friendship with his new classmates and Form Teachers as they prepare to forge ahead together in the year 2026.

The details of the camp are as follows:

Date: **12th to  15th January (Monday to Thursday)**

Venue: **Jalan Bahtera MOE Outdoor Adventure Learning Centre (OALC)**

Reporting on **12th January 2026, 7:30 am @ School**

Reporting attire: **PE attire**

Cost: Nil

Packing list for the camp will be given to your son and more information will be disseminated via Parents Gateway nearer to date.

Parents will be invited to attend the badge ceremony on **15th January at 10:30am (in school)**.

**16th January, Friday**, will be **Wellness Day** for Sec 1 Students to rest at home after the Camp.
