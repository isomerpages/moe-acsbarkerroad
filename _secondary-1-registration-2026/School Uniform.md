<h4><strong>Dress Code</strong></h4>
<hr>
<p>Please note the following for required attire and appearance for all our
Lower Secondary students:</p>
<p><strong>Uniform</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>The badge is to be pinned 2 finger widths above the shirt pocket.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The badge is to be centred over the pocket.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The badge is to hang straight at all times.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School shirt to be neatly tucked in at all times.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The sleeves of the school shirt cannot be folded up. If they are too long,
appropriate alteration can be done.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only school-approved jacket/windcheater may be worn.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The ACS(BR) bermudas is to be worn.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The bermudas should be snug-fitting, such that the waistline of the bermudas
is no more than 2 finger widths below the navel.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Shoes should be mostly white with white shoelaces. For any coloured parts,
only non-striking colours are allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only plain white socks or socks bearing ‘ACS(BR)’ may be worn.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Socks are to be pulled up properly to their entire lengths and they are
to cover the ankles fully.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Personal Grooming</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Beards and moustaches are strictly not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hair is to be short and neatly combed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sides and back of hair is to be sloped and not touch the ears and collar.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Fringe should not cross the eyebrows when pulled down.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The sideburns should be kept short.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use of gel/wax for ‘spiking’ of hair is strictly not allowed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Extreme hairstyles (include and are not limited to, Under-cut, V-shaped
back, Mohawk, Skinhead, Pompadour hairstyles and lines cut into hair) and
use of hair colouring are not allowed.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Eye-wear</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Spectacles, if worn, are to be of a basic and functional purpose.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Recommended frame colours are black, dark blue, brown, gold or silver.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Coloured contact lenses are not to be worn.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Piercings</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Piercings of ears and wearing of any accessories on the ears is not permitted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Piercing of and wearing of accessories around the facial regions is not
permitted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Accessories</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Recommended colours for watches are black or silver.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wearing of any other accessories on the wrist or fingers is not allowed.</p>
</li>
</ul>
