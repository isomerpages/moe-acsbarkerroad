<p>Could ACS (Barker Road) be the school for you?</p>
<p>If you are a Primary 5 or 6 student with a passion for Sports, Performing
Arts, or Clubs &amp; Societies, we warmly invite you and your parents to
join us at our DSA Engagement Session 2026. Come and discover the Direct
School Admission pathway, explore our distinctive programmes, and get a
feel for the brotherhood, values, and spirit that make ACS (Barker Road)
truly special.</p>
<p>Hear from our School Leaders about what sets ACS (Barker Road) apart.
Listen to the stories of our current DSA students — boys just like you
— who took that first step and never looked back. Then meet our CCA teachers
and find out how you can begin your own ACS journey.</p>
<p>Your story at ACS (Barker Road) could start here. We would love to see
you!</p>
<p><strong>Date</strong>:&nbsp;Friday, 15 May 2026&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Time</strong>:&nbsp;5.00 pm – 6.30 pm&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Venue</strong>:&nbsp;ACS (Barker Road) Auditorium</p>
<p><strong>Programme Highlights</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Address by School Leaders</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sharing by Current DSA Students</p>
</li>

<p>Check-in Session with DSA CCA Teachers</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong><u>Important Information</u></strong>
</p>
<p>This <strong>DSA Engagement Session</strong> will replace our usual Open
House this year.</p>
<p>Due to limited seating capacity, only <strong>registered visitors </strong>will
be allowed entry into the auditorium for the <strong>School Leaders’ address and engagement session with our current DSA students</strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Walk-in visitors without prior registration</strong> may still
join us from <strong>6.00pm to 6.30pm</strong> to check in with our <strong>DSA CCA teachers</strong> and
find out more about the individual talent areas.</p>
<p>As places are limited, registration is on a <strong>first-come, first-served basis</strong>.
We are currently just a few seats away from full capacity, so do register
early to secure your spot.</p>
