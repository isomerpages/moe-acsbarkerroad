### **School Booklist 2026**

Parents are strongly encouraged to purchase through the school bookshop or pre-order via the website at [https://www.pacificbookstores.com](https://www.pacificbookstores.com/) to ensure correct edition of books.

**Online Purchase**<br>
Place your order at [https://www.pacificbookstores.com](https://www.pacificbookstores.com/). You may choose between Home Delivery and Self-Collection at the school bookshop.

**Walk In**<br>
You may purchase from the school bookshop on the dates stated in the booklist.
