### **Financial Support**

| Name of FAS | Quantum of FAS/Bursary | Eligibility Criteria | Additional Information | Application Process |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **MOE-Financial Assistance Scheme (MOE-FAS)** | • 100% fee subsidies<br><br>• Free textbooks<br><br>• Free school attire<br><br>•	Meal subsidies for 10 meals per week<br><br>•	Monthly public transport subsidies | • Student must be a Singapore citizen enrolled in a Government or Government-aided school and meets either one of the following criteria:<br><br>- Family’s average Gross Household Income (GHI) over 12 months does not exceed **$4,000** per month; or<br><br>- Per Capita Income (PCI)* does not exceed **$1000**.<br><br>* *PCI = Monthly GHI / No. of Household Members* | • Eligible for consideration for additional financial assistance where required<br><br>• Awardees will be expected to<br><br>- Conduct themselves well<br><br>- Contribute to the school community<br><br>- Perform reasonably well academically | Parents may apply through either of the following options:<br><br>•	Submit application electronically via https://go.gov.sg/moe-efas or scan the QR code provided below<br><br>•	Collect the Application Form from the General Office and submit it together with the required supporting documents:<br><br>- CPF Statements<br><br>- Income Tax  Notice of Assessment<br><br>- Pay slips<br><br>- Declarations of unemployment (if applicable) |
|
