<h3>Group Personal Accident (GPA)&nbsp;Insurance for Students</h3>
<p>All students are covered under GPA Insurance that provides protection
for accidents occurring during school activities, on school premises, and
whilst commuting to and from school.
<br>For more information, please refer to the <a href="/files/Product_Fact_Sheet__Year_2026_.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Product Factsheet</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Claims Procedures</strong>
</p>
<p>All claims must be submitted online through the <a href="https://studentgpa.incomegroupins.com.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Student GPA Claims Portal</a>.</p>
