<h3>Group Personal Accident (GPA)&nbsp;Insurance for Students</h3>
<p>All students are covered under GPA Insurance that provides protection
for accidents occurring during school activities, on school premises, and
whilst commuting to and from school. For more information, please refer
to the <strong><a href="/files/Product_Fact_Sheet__Year_2026_.pdf" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Product Factsheet</a></strong>.</p>
<p><strong>Claims Procedures</strong>
</p>
<p>All claims must be submitted online through the <strong><a href="https://studentgpa.incomegroupins.com.sg/#/disclaimer" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Student GPA Claims Portal</a></strong>.</p>
